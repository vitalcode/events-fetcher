Capability Brown 'Glimpses of Ickworth'

Exhibition

National Trust Ickworth
Horringer
Bury St Edmunds
Suffolk
IP29 5QE

Tel: 01284 735270

Click here to email

Click here to visit website

Capability Brown’s involvement at Ickworth has always been intriguing.  We know he visited and was paid the sum of around £700, but there is very little evidence of his work, it is thought he recommended the site for the new house. As part of the Tercentenary celebrations, the Colne and Colchester, Suffolk East and Waveney branches of the Embroiderer’s Guild have produced a display of unique textile work based around typically English scenes of landscapes and stately gardens inspired by Ickworth, the seasons and Capability Brown.  The display entitled ‘Glimpses of Ickworth’ comprises seven large-scale, banners using a variety of embroidery techniques.  The banners represent hours of work by Guild members over the last 6 months and are fantastically detailed.
