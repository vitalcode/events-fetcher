	Master Drawings Exhibition Lecture The Invention of Drawing
Type	Conferences And Exhibitions

Description	In the 15th century the art of drawing became the foundation of all other arts. The uses of drawing in this period explain much about the momentous changes that we associate with painting in Renaissance Italy. This lecture will describe the new approach to drawing and how it has affected the practice of painting from then until the present day.
Location	Ashmolean Museum of Art and Archaeology
