Teddy Time at Milestones
Basingstoke area
Milestones Museum

Fun for all the family at this Teddy themed event! Can you bear to miss it?! 
Bring your teddy for a special day out at Milestones, fun activities and workshops on a teddy bear theme. See our vintage teddy collection, storytelling, ongoing trails and more!
Some activities may be subject to an extra charge.
Closed Mondays, except Bank Holidays.
