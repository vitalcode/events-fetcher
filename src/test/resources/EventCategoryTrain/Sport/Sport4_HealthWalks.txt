Health Walks in St Ives

About this activity

As part of the Huntingdonshire Health Walks Scheme, walks take place in St Ives every Tuesday afternoon at 2.15pm and Thursday mornings at 10am using various routes. All walks are free of charge and are led by trained volunteer walk leaders. Join us today and walk your way to a better health. 

For further information contact the Sport and Active Lifestyles Team on 01480 387047
