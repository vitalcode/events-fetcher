The Mary Hayton Charity Football Day

A day of football matches as well as lots of raffle prizes to be won.
Two children's football matches, an over 40's match & Sacriston WMC V Langley Park WMC play for The Mary Hayton Charity Shield. There will also be a raffle, quiz & bingo throughout the day. Raffle prizes include an iPad, 10 driving lessons & signed football shirts!

Tickets are £3 for the daytime and £5 for the evening and can be purchased from Eileen Jones on 0191 3734959 or 07415900886.
