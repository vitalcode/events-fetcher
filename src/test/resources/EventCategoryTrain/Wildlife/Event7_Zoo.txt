The Banham Zoo Brilliantly Barmy Bugs event will be on during 1st – 31st August at Banham Zoo, a great day out during the August Bank Holiday.

This summer during the August Bank Holiday, we are encouraging bug love at Banham Zoo to tie in with BIAZA’s insect week! Find out about some of Britain’s creepy crawlies and flying insects during our bug hunt and hold some exciting exotic creatures too! Make your own bizarre little beastie and have a bug face paint, part Banham’s Brilliantly Barmy Bugs event!

For more information about visiting Banham’s Brilliantly Barmy Bugs event during the August Bank Holiday, please visit the Banham Zoo attraction page.
