may bank holiday half term, pennywell farm


The May Bank Holiday Half Term event will be on 27th to 31st May 2013 during Easter Half Term at the Pennywell Farm in Devon.
May Bank Holiday Half Term event will be packed full of half term fun and family entertainment with Nutty Noah, Dan the Hat and Ben Cornish.
During The May Bank Holiday Half Term event, all the fabulous activities that Pennywell is famous for are on offer every half hour. Race the Ferrets, go pond dipping, cuddle a piglet or join in with tortoise teatime.
For more information about visiting Pennywell Farm during May, please visit the Pennywell Farm attraction page.
