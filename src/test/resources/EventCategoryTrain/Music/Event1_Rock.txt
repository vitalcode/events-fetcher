Cambridge Rock Festival,
A superb Rock Festival featuring mainly Rock and Blues, with added Prog, NWBHM, great food and 70 real ales, ciders, wines and lager! Camping and caravanning available over the full festival. Just 2.5 miles from the historic centre of Cambridge and 3.3miles from Cambridge Rail Station. 30 mins from Stansted Airport (150metres from M11 Motorway J12). Cambridge Rocks! as after all Cambridge is the original hometown of Pink Floyd.
