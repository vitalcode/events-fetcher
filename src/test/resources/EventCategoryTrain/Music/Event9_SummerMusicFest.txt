Festival Chorus
East Anglia Chamber Orchestra
Johannes Brahms German Requiem

This community-based Festival Chorus event brings together members of local choirs, both ‘town and gown’, and the East Anglia Chamber Orchestra in celebratory mood, in the glorious surroundings of King's College Chapel. This is the fifth in our successful Festival Chorus concerts.
