Cambridge Guitar Ensemble
a group for guitarists who like playing music together
for guitarists with some experience – not suitable for complete beginners
have fun with these fun ensemble playing sessions
you need a Spanish, classical or acoustic guitar to join in
reading from standard music notation
improve your guitar skills, music knowledge and understanding
sheet music provided beforehand so you can try it at home
The ensemble playing sessions are at St James Centre, Wulfstan Way, Cambridge CB1 8QJ 
Entrance to events is three pounds. Under sixteens must be with an adult.
This entrance includes Cambridge Guitar Club.
