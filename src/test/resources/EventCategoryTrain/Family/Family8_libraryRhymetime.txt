Rhymetime



(term time only) Come along and join the fun of singing and rhyming! Borrow books from the library! Every Wednesday afternoon we run two repeated sessions at 2.00pm and 2.30pm at Cherry Hinton Library. No booking required just pop along! Sessions are free. Suitable for babies and toddlers up to 5 years of age.

Website: http://www.cambridgeshirechildrenscentres.org.uk/
