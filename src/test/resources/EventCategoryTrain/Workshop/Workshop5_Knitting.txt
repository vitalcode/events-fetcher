
What’s On  
Knitting flowers
Susie Johns 

Spend a day making beautiful blooms using yarn and knitting needles. Susie will show you how to create a range of popular garden flowers, including lilies, daffodils, cosmos, periwinkle and grape hyacinth. This workshop is suitable for all knitters, regardless of experience: you just need knowledge of basic stitches. With individual and group tuition, you will be able to produce your own knitted posy. Date 23/05/2013 Time 10am – 4pm Price £50 Booking required
