One Stop Nature Bird Walk
Where To Meet: Meet at The One Stop Nature Shop before heading onto the marsh which is less than a 2 minute walk away.

Time and Duration: The walk starts at 5:15pm and will last for around 2 hours. We will walk along the sea wall at Burnham Deepdale where there is good views to be had of local birds.

Parking: Parking is available outside The One Stop Nature Shop

What to bring:
Sunblock advised on warm days as there is no cover along the sea wall, walking boots also advisable.
Binoculars will be useful. They can be hired from The One Stop Nature Shop in advance at £5 for the walk. Please contact us on 01485 211223. No dogs please!

Booking: Please let The One Stop Nature Shop know that you would like to attend.
