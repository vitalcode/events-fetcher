Hey Boy Hey Girl is your only chance to experience the kind of party that defines Cambridge. Swapping the current minimalist movement with a much more hedonistic approach, and mixing both classic and contemporary, bass heavy break-beats, house, hip hop and jungle. Hey Boy Hey Girl is your call to the dancefloor. And there is no better place to stage this than the Cambridge Fez. Straight up euphoric moments surrounded by carefree like minded people
