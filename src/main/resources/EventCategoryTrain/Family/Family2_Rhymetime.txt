WE'RE GOING ON A BEAR HUNT 

JUSTINE DE MIERRE

Justine de Mierre
Join professional storyteller Justine de Mierre for a celebration of the classic picture book, We’re going on a Bear Hunt. This is a fun, interactive event for under 5s and their grownups including games, songs and activities and ending with an interactive telling of the story. Come and swish through the grass, squelch through the mud and run away from the bears with us! 

 

Age 2+ (maximum of two children per adult recommended)
