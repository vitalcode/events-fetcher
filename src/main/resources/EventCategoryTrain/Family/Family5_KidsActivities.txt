YOUNG EXPLORERS

Description	Let your children follow our map and join in the scavenger hunt around our gardens. Finish the trail and become an official Mount Stuart Young Explorer. 

Mount Stuart Gardens are full of surprises with ponds, waterfalls, beaches, amazing plants and stunning views. Follow the clues to uncover some of its hidden natural gems and secrets. Get to know the team by asking them to provide clues and point the way to ensure you gain all the answers on the scavenger hunt. Complete the trail and your child will be presented with a special keepsake of their day at Mount Stuart. 

Special requirements: children must be accompanied by an adult.
Location	Mount Stuart House and Gardens
