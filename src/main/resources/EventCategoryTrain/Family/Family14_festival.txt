Moshi Monsters at Festival Leisure Park!


The Moshi Monsters are coming to Festival Leisure Park this May and you could meet them!

Katsuma and Poppet have taken a short break from their latest mission on the streets of Monstro City to visit Festival Leisure Park on Thursday 30th May and will be appearing at intervals between 11am and 4pm... Plus, there’s free face painting for the kids!
