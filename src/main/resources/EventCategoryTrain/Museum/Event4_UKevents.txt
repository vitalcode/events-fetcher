OPEN STUDIOS WEEKEND: TRISKELE DESIGNS
Type	Conferences And Exhibitions

Description	Come along and watch Jessica Herriot, a local ceramic artist bring her porcelain jugs, bowls and plates to life with the delicate stroke of a brush. 

Jessica Herriot from Triskele Designs will be on site demonstrating ceramic decoration. Each piece has its own identity, hand painted with vibrant, on glaze colours, she draws her inspiration from the wild flowers on the Inch and Bute. 

Commissioned work will be welcomed on the day.
Location	Mount Stuart House and Gardens
Price	Free
