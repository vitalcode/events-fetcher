Jowett Car Club Rally at Milestones
Basingstoke area
Milestones Museum

On the 25th and 26th May the oldest one make car club in the world, the Jowett Car Club, celebrates its 90th Anniversary by bringing hundreds of cars and their owners from across Europe to the Milestones Museum. With classic cars built between 1910 to 1954 and activities for all the family, come and enjoy a day out with a difference, and learn about the history of car manufacturing in the UK

Cost: Normal entry for museum
Venue: Milestones Museum
Promoter: Jowett Car Club
