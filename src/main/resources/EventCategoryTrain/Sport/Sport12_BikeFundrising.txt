London to Cambridge Bike Ride

Starting from Pickett's Lock in north London, the ride winds through beautiful countryside to the finish at Midsummer Common in the heart of Cambridge, where you'll be met with music, refreshments and massage. 

If you'd like to buy one of the new Breakthrough Breast Cancer cycling jerseys, email activeevents@breakthrough.org.uk with the size and quantity you'd like, along with your name, address and phone number. They are £30 each incl VAT (see example picture at the bottom right on this page).
