Historical bike tour around Cambridge tomorrow

Cyclists across Cambridge are being invited to get on their bikes this week and take part in an unusual bike tour around the historic sites of the local area.

 

The tours, organised by Cycle Cambridge, offer the chance to cycle past historical sites throughout Cambridge and enjoy some of the excellent local green spaces from the Market Square through to Cherry Hinton Hall.

 

Hosted by blue badge tour guide and local expert Allan Brigham, the tours start on Wednesday 16th June and cost just £3.

 

Tickets are available from The Cambridge Tourist Information Centre in Peas Hill.
