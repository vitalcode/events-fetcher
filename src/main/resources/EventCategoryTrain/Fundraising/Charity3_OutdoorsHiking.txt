Hiking4Life

Take on a spectacular 50km hike across South Western Crete to raise vital funds for Cancer Research UK.
Join Cancer Research UK supporters, Pamela and Antony, on a stunning 50km hike through Crete, taking in two beautiful gorges in the south western part of the island.

This is a 'pay as you go' trip, but costs can be provided to help with budgeting. Reservations will be made for rooms, breakfast and evening meals for the duration of the hike.

This event is organised by a supporter of Cancer Research UK, please read our terms and conditions.
