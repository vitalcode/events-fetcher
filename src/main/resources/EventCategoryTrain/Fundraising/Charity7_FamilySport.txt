Virgin Money Cyclone 2013
The Virgin Money Cyclone Festival of Cycling has something for everyone, from complete novices to expert cyclists.

Tyne 6 Bridges - a wonderful leisure ride along the banks of the Tyne for novice cyclists and families.

Cyclone Challenge - starting at Newcastle Falcons Rugby Club and taking in the beautiful Northumberland countryside, this ride is aimed at non competetive cyclists looking to test their fitness and set themselves a personal challenge.

Cycle in this fantastic event for Cancer Research UK and help raise money for our life-saving research.
